Potato and Leek Soup

This soup receipe is great for cold and dreary days

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Bullion
  • Heavy Cream
  • Leek
  • Potato

Instructions

  1. Place a large pot on the stove on medium heat.
  2. Melt the butter in the pot, brown garlic.
  3. Chop leeks and place them in the pot.
  4. Cut up potatoes into cubes.
  5. Place potatoes in the pot.
  6. Pour in cream, boil for 20 minutes.
