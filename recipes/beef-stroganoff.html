Beef Stroganoff

This stroganoff is made with Impossible Beef tips and is one of my favorite vegetarian meals. This is creamy and filling, the perfect comfort food for any day. Works best when served over egg noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1 package Impossible Beef Tips
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 4 oz butter
  • 4 green onions
  • 4 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp no chicken bullion
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 6 oz sliced mushrooms
  • 1/3 cup sour cream

Instructions

  1. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add beef tips and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Push the beef to the side and add green onions. Cook and stir for 3 to 5 minute.
  4. Pour in the broth and stir with the flour. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Stir in mushrooms and sour cream. Cook until heated through. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve over hot noodles.
