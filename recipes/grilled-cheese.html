Grilled Cheese

A grilled cheese sandwich is one of my favorite comfort foods. This is super easy to make and is so delicious. Pairs well with a tomato soup.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • 2 Tbsp Salted Butter
  • 1 Slice of Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 Slice of Pepper Jack Cheese

Instructions

  1. Heat up a skillet on medium heat.
  2. Butter each side of the slices of bread.
  3. Place a slice of bread on the skillet.
  4. Place each slice of cheese on the bread.
  5. Cook for about 2 minutes until the bread is golden brown, then flip.
  6. Cook the other side for another 2 minutes or until golden brown.
  7. Transfer to a plate and enjoy!
